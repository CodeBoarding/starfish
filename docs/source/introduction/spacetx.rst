SpaceTx
============

*SpaceTx* is a consortium of groups developing assays for image-based
transcriptomics.

SpaceTx Assays
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

We are currently developing pipelines for each of image-based
transcriptomics assays that are being developed by the SpaceTx groups.
